Why NMD

I chose to study the New media Program because I wanted to study something that is both practical and theoretical and gives you the opportunity to work in another country. It was also important to me to choose a program that included an internship and the possibility of a semester abroad. But most of all I chose this program because it seemed fun and like something that would fit me well.
