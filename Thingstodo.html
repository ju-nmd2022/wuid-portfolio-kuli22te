Things to do in Jönköping

Dumme mosse

Dumme mosse is a nature reserv where
you can take a walk on planked
trails or bird watch.

555 93 Jönköping

Rudenstams Gårdsbutik

Rudenstams farm shop sells localy produced
meat, fish and chesses as well as self-produced
fruits, berries and drinks made from the goods.
They also have a cafe where you can enjoy
a lunch or a cup of coffe.

Lillsjöraden 12,, 553 20 Jönköping
